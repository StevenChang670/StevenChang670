## Personal Research Notes

The following notes are available on my [GitHub](https://github.com/StevenChang670/Physics-Notes).

- **Quantum Field Theory**: This is my self-study note on quantum field theory. I aim to establish consistent notations and begin from a fundamental perspective, namely the representation theory of the Lorentz group. The notes also cover scattering theory and the optical theorem.
- **In-In Formalism**: I review the in-in formalism, beginning with a review of the fundamentals of the traditional in-out path integral. I then explain the motivation for the in-in formalism, derive several of its properties, and organize the different types of Green functions.
- **Scattering Theory of Quantum Mechanics**: I provide a comprehensive review of scattering theory in quantum mechanics, organizing the material into the operator formalism and the differential equations framework.
